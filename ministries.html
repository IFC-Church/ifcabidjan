Ministries Info

Sunday School.

Meets every Sunday, upstairs at 9:00 AM - 11:00 Am for Children (Ages: 0-11) & Youths (Ages: 12-19) for their Sunday school activities.

  • Every Sunday
  • 8:30AM - 8:55PM
  • Church Auditorium

Men's Ministry.

Meets for 'Hour of Power' prayer on the first and third Saturday at the church. Thier montly is every last Sunday of every month immediately after service.

  • Last Sundays of every month
  • Immediately after service
  • Church Premises

Women's Ministry.

Meets online for Bible study & prayer every Monday from 7:00pm - 8:15pm

  • Every Monday
  • 7:00PM - 8:15PM
  • online

Prayer Ministry.

Meets on the last Fridays montly for a vigil at the Church premises. Come and be blessed

  • Last Friday of the every month
  • 9:00PM - dawn
  • Church premises

Married ministry.

Meets for fellowship on the second Sunday of every month after service at IFC. It also organizes quarterly marriage seminars. The Ministry exists to support building marriages according to God's Blueprint (Bible).

  • 2nd sunday of every month
  • Church premises

Evangelism/Outreach Ministry.

The outreach ministry meets first Sunday of the month after church service for one hour prayer and intercession for sould. Please join us even if you are not a core member of the ministry.

  • 1st Sunday of every month
  • After church service
  • 12-13 years old class
