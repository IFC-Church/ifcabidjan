Welcome to IFC

Welcome to the International Fellowship of Christians (IFC) Church. We are an English-speaking international Church with a goal to serve all people from all works of life. Your presence in our services is cherished and appreciated. We look forward to meeting you to know that we care. Please allow us to be a blessing to you and your family through having fellowship with us.
